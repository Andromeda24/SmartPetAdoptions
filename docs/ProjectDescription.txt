"Not every pet is right for every person,
 and not every home is right for every pet. 
 If your pet had Tinder, would it choose you? 
 Let Smart Pet Adoption do the job."

Name: Smart Pet Adoption

Description: This application will help shelter staff find appropriate homes 
for their animals by taking into account the character of the animals, 
the preferences of pet seekers, and the pets they already own


Use cases:
            SignUp
            Login
            isAuthorized Middleware
            listPets
            addPet
            deletePet
            updatePet
            adminUserProfile
            recomendPet:Use AI to match pet seeker preferences with the character of 
                    available animals to generate a recommendation list
